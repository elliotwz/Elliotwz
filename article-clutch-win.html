Ability to Perform in the Clutch Gives Oregon Its First Conference Win of the Season

The energy in Matthew Knight Arena was electric Friday night as the Ducks stepped onto their home floor for the first time in 26 days.

In a five-set nail-biter, the student section roared as junior outside hitter Valentina Vaulet fired the ball onto the floor and sealed the win for Oregon.

The hard-fought final set ended by a score of 19-17.
