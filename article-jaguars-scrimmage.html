Willamette Valley Jaguars Season Ends With 137-130 Scrimmage Win

What was supposed to be the final game of the Willamette Valley Jaguars' season versus the Seattle Super Hawks quickly turned into a friendly scrimmage when the Super Hawks' team bus broke down on their way to Corvallis.

The change of plans happened only two hours before game time, and a temporary scrimmage team was quickly assembled to give the Jags one last home game.

Made up of former Jaguar players, friends, and even team owner Marion Clark, the scrimmage team was named Fresh.
