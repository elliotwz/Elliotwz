About

Background and Direction

My name is Elliot Watts-Zagha, and sports have been my passion for as long as I can remember. That is what brought me to the University of Oregon, where I am currently a third-year Journalism student with a minor in Sports Business.

My journey into broadcasting started on campus. I have gotten hands-on experience in a lot of different areas, from reporting and scriptwriting to calling games to working behind the camera on live productions.

Every role has been different and has pushed me to keep growing. Looking ahead, I am ready to go wherever the road takes me.

Focus

  • Anchoring and reporting
  • Broadcast writing and script development
  • Play-by-play and color commentary
  • Live production and multimedia storytelling
